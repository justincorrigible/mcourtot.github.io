GA4GH Connect

Mélanie Courtot Director Genome Informatics & Principal Investigator
April 27, 2024 October 29, 2024
GA4GH meetings

Last week Jon Eubank, technical associate director in the Genome Informatics team and I had the pleasure to attend the GA4GH Connect meeting in Ascona, Switzerland.

We heard and contributed to different sessions around implementing Beacon for ICGC-ARGO and the Pan Canadian Genome Library (PCGL), as well as work the Regulatory and Ethics working group is initiating. Jon talked about our work on Overture and I presented the new PCGL project to the Steering Committee, highlighting connections between PCGL and GA4GH, with my colleagues David Bujold and Ma’n Zawaati. Slides for the PCGL GA4GH intro are available here.

Ascona itself was beautiful and the Swiss/Italian charm did not disappoint!
